Greenvault Electrical represents the pinnacle of coastal luxury living in Cape Town. Our challenge was to create a digital experience that matches the sophistication of the architecture while driving real estate inquiries.

The Challenge

The existing website was outdated, slow, and failed to capture the premium nature of the development. Mobile experience was poor, and the inquiry process was cumbersome, leading to lost leads.

Our Solution

A lightning-fast, mobile-first platform with immersive visuals, interactive floor plans, and a streamlined inquiry system. Integrated with CRM for instant lead capture and follow-up.
